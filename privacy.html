Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

At SnapGlow, accessible from SnapGlow.app (or your domain), one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by SnapGlow and how we use it.

1. No Data Storage (Client-Side Only)

SnapGlow operates entirely in your web browser (client-side). When you paste code, notes, or generate images, your data is never sent to our servers. All image generation processes happen locally on your device. We do not store, view, or share your content.

2. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, SnapGlow uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

3. Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of a third-party vendor on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to www.website.com and other sites on the internet. However, visitors may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at the following URL – https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Third Party Privacy Policies

SnapGlow's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. Thus, we are advising you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information.

5. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its Terms and Conditions.
